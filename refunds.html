Refund Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

7-Day Hassle-Free Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are unhappy with your item, you can return it within 7 days of receiving the delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Conditions for Returns

  • Items must be unused and in the exact condition you received them.
  • Items must be in their original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Proof of purchase (order receipt or email) is required.
  • Personalized, perishable, or final sale items are not eligible for returns.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Late or missing refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at support@swcart.example.com.
